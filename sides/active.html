Our mission

We are a group of animals’ rights activists with the same purpose: to use our voice for those who do not have one. We have been been spreading the message individually for over a decade, until we finally met each other at different times and places, then came to the realization of an enormous gap in people’s knowledge about daily life foods, such as where milk or gelatin come from. However, we wanted to do something about it, so we gathered a group a different professionals who can provide scientific studies, and proof of the information we want to share with everyone. The reason of it all is, there is huge amount of economic resources invested by the meat, and dairy industries to provide information meant to benefit these industries, most of the time supported by pseudoscientific studies paid by these very same industries, regardless of the effects on the environment nor for the people’s health.
